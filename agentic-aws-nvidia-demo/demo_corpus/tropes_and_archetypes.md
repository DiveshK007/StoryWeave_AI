# Tropes and Archetypes
## Archetypes
- Protagonist: Clear goal and flaw.
- Antagonist: Opposes goal with credible motive.
- Mentor: Gives tools or wisdom.
- Trickster: Injects chaos or misdirection.
## Tropes
- Fish out of water
- Found family
- Redemption arc
- Ticking clock
- Heist with a twist
Guidelines: Keep motivations consistent. Escalate conflict every beat.
